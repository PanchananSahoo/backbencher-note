Computer Science and Information Technology

B.Tech in Computer Science and Information Technology is designed for candidates seeking engineering excellence in the field of Computer Software and Hardware Technology.Our lives would be unrecognizable without computing, Information systems make Industries run in all the fields.

Our future looks fascinating how it works is down to you.Software Development, Software testing, Website design, network security, robotics, Intelligence learning, Neural Network, Database Administration, Social media, face recognition, Biomedical Development, IOT.

Vision
  • Preparing technologists with in-depth insights into information technology, and embedding ethics via focused technical training.
Mission
  • Empower technologists to excel in information technology through rigorous training and hands-on experience.
  • Foster a culture of integrity and responsibility by instilling ethical principles in every aspect of technical education.
  • Encourage technologists with new ideas and good leadership in the tech world, training to possess strong values.
